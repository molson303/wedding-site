THE BRIDE

OUR STORY

Being honest here, we both don’t remember meeting each other for the first time back in 2012, so the title of this section should really be, “we met before we really met.” But here is a little story about how we both remember meeting in 2014, which we later came to find, was for the second time, we will fill you in on the details...

DEC
2014
THE BEGINNING

On a snowy night in December of 2014, while Maddie was on winter break, she got a call from her friend Kyle to come over and hang out at his friend’s (Mike's) Breckenridge rental ski house right down the street from her family’s home. Being so close by, she had no excuse and trecked down the road. Upon walking in the door, Maddie barely made it up the stairs as she was greeted by some very large, slobbery dogs. But despite the altitude, steep stairs, and the puppies, she persevered and made it to the summit, to find a man wearing an argyle sweater, fake glasses, and a smiley face hat.

DEC
2014
THE NUMBER EXCHANGE

As the night continued, everyone at the house wanted to move the party downtown to the bars. Maddie, responsible and studious, was the designated driver for that night and that is how she scored Mike's phone number. Mike wanting to make sure he could see Maddie again put that smiley face hat on her head and let her borrow it for the night. But in all reality, Mike just wanted a reason to call her the next day, and hopefully, have a new ski buddy.

After this initial encounter, their friendship continued to grow on the slopes of Breckenridge as ski adventure buddies. Maddie worked hard to keep up with Mike in the trees, while Mike constantly tried to convince Maddie to be his Tbar partner.

JAN
2015
FIRST DATE

After many trips of the chair lifts together, a night of traditional Fondue at the Swiss Haven in Breckenridge, and an afternoon adventure snowshoeing, Maddie asked Mike on a formal date. It was then mutually decided it would be fun to surprise each other with special first dates.

So after Maddie asked Mike on this date, it was decided it would be fun to surprise one another with a special first dates.

Maddie’s first date for Mike:
Because of Mike’s love for beer, Maddie took him to Prost Brewery in the Highlands neighboorhood to test his taste buds. She blindfolded him, got him a fight of beers, and by smell only he had to decipher the flavors, type, and ABV of the beers. She then took him to dinner at the trendy Root Down restaurant, and to finish the night, Maddie presented a special dessert she had created. Knowing Mike’s love of ice cream cake and that his favorite color was blue, she crafted these innovative blue ice-cream cake cupcakes.

Mike’s first date for Maddie:
Mike made reservations to take Maddie to the famous Boulder, Colorado Oak restaurant. Unfortunately, on their arrival, the restaurant had a fire, so they improvised and went to Ted’s Montana grill. After dinner, Mike revealed his big surprise adventure and took Maddie to a “Sip and Paint” event, where they drank wine and attempted to paint Van Gogh’s Cafe Terrace at Night. After showing off their elementary paint skills to each other, they finished the night with their mutual favorite dessert: Mochi!

DEC
2012
HOW WE REALLY FIRST MET

Upon reactivating Mike’s twitter account, in anticipation for Maddie to track his mileage for his first half marathon in the Fall of 2018, a one of a kind green tie-dye ski jacket was spotted in one of his tweets from 2012. Because of that jacket, Maddie knew that was her in the picture, and both her and Mike sat staring at the photo and date in disbelief. This Epic Mix photo (aka- photo from a camera man on the ski hill) showing a picture of Kyle, Brandon, Mike, Maddie and a few others from 2012 is evidence that these two had met before, and luckily fate brought them back together again in the snowy town of Breckenridge.

JAN
2015
CONTINUATION

Maddie, still in undergrad had to head back to Tacoma, WA to finish her last year. Mike not wanting to let her go flew out 1-2x a month to see her. This was a very special time, as Mike was able to experience Tacoma with Maddie, attend her formal events, and also see her walk during graduation.

SEPT
2018
ENGAGEMENT

September 8, 2018-The best day ever...until June 6, 2020.
Unbeknown to Maddie, Mike and his mother had been spending almost every weekend for the last few months looking for the perfect engagement ring. After months of shopping, lunch dates, digital and wax mockups Mike and his mother finally created the perfect ring. With Maddie's birthday right around the corner, Mike decided to surprise her with what she thought was an early birthday weekend escape in Vail, CO. As they left Denver to head up to the mountains, they received a call from Barbara who wanted to meet up to give Maddie her birthday gift, a backpack Yetti cooler with picknick items the two could enjoy during their hike. With the gift in hand, the couple, along with Rockie, drove up to Beaver Creek for a day of hiking in the mountains. Hiking for over 2 hours, they got near the top of Beaver Creek, on the Red Buffalo ski run, and with their tummies growling they decided to investigate what was in this cooler.
The very first thing packed at the top of the cooler was a blindfold labeled for Maddie. With her face up the mountain, blindfolded, Mike prepared Rockie with a bandana, set up the picnic scene, and got on one knee. Turning around, Maddie faced her soon to be husband, on his knee, holding a ring while Rockie, wore a "Will you marry me" bandana.

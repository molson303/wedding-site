Born and raised in Colorado, I grew up skiing with my family at Copper Mountain. As a kid, I spent a lot of time outdoors: camping, hiking, playing sports, and riding bikes. Wanting to stay in Colorado after high school, I attended the University of Colorado where I received my B.A. in Economics. After a few years working for Vail Resorts and then Leanin' Tree, I made a decision to go back to school to learn Web Development and Software Engineering. I proceeded to land a job with a startup company in Denver that connects talent with companies. I have always been an athletic person and over the last two years found a new love for endurance sports. When I am not coding you can find me running, swimming, hiking, skiing, riding my bike, or giving Rockie belly rubs.

THE BRIDE

Hi! I'm Maddie! I was born and raised in CO. My brother and I grew up spending our time outside. In the winters you'd find us skiing down the slopes of Breckenridge, or getting stuck in the deep powder on our snowmobiles in the backyard. In the summers, you'd find us playing any sport we could to be outside. At a young age, after trying most sports, I fell in love with soccer and that passion took me to the University of Puget Sound, in WA. While playing soccer for UPS, I earned my B.S. in Molecular and Cellular Biology. After graduating from college, I moved back to Colorado to pursue a Masters in Business Administration degree in my year before starting medical school. During this time working on my MBA, I can not forget to mention the very important moment in my life when I got Rockie, my amazing, best friend, Golden Retriever. After medical school I landed my residency at the University of Minnesota where I am now a Neurology Resident. In short, I am a fun-loving, bubbly, outdoor adventuring, neurology-loving, dog person.

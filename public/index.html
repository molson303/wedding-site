THE STORY

Being honest here, we both don’t remember meeting each other for the first time December 20th, 2012, so the title of this section should really be, “we met before we really met.” But here is a little story about how we both remember meeting, which we later came to find, was for the second time, we will fill you in on the details.
DEC
2014
The Beginning

On a snowy night in December of 2014, while Maddie was on college break, she got a call from her friend Kyle to come over and hang out at his friend’s (Mike's) Breckenridge rental ski house, which happened to be right down the street from her family’s vacation home. Not wanting to stop watching Austen Powers with her dad she managed to head over to the house. Walking in the door, Maddie barely made it up the stairs as she was greeted by some very large, slobbery dogs. But despite the altitude, steep stairs, and the puppies, she persevered and made it to the summit, to find a man wearing an argyle sweater, fake glasses, and a smiley face on his hat. The couple hit it off from first sight. This was what we thought was the first time we had ever met.

SEP
2015
The Number Exchange

As the night continued, the house mates all wanted to head to the breckenrige bars. Maddie, responsible and studious, was the designated driver for that night and that is how she (thinks) she scored Mike's phone number. Mike wanting to make sure he could see maddie again put that smiley face hat on maddie's head and let he borrow it for the night. But in all reality, Mike just wanted a reason to call Maddie the next day, and hopefully go skiing.

After this initial encounter, their friendship continued to grow on the slopes of Breckenridge, as ski buddies. Maddie worked hard to keep up with Mike in the trees, while Mike tried to convience Maddie to be his Tbar partner.
